WRT commentary, I think I am with Stroustrup here, meaning that the  
commentary is good to fill in what *cannot* be better said in code.  
Instead, comments should provide a general idea of what a piece of code  
does, and possibly describe the interfaces and return values, at least the  
special cases (if any) such as NULL, -1, 0, ...  descriptive and accurate  
variable and function names also reduce the need for commentary in epic  
breadth. :-)
