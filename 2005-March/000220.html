This was a problem for me, downloading to laptop on the go and desktop where
I kept everything archived. I finally solved it by downloading to whichever
I happened to be on, keeping all my messages in Maildir format (so each
message is in a separate file), and then using 'unison' to synchronise the
two home directories from time to time. Works beautifully, and also means my
laptop is fully backed up to my desktop PC, and vice versa.
