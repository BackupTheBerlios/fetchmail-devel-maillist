Greetings,

Just as an advance warning for packagers:

I have just removed the NET_SECURITY macro, the --netsec/-T options, the
--enable-netsec configure option and all code pertinent to this from
fetchmail[conf] and its documentation (as of revision 4192 in the
Subversion repository), as the required library, Craig Metz's
inet6-apps, is no longer available and it was insufficiently documented
and just cluttered up our code.

If you have questions, concerns, comments, objections, feel free to
follow up.

Regards,
