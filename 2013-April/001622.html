As the fetchmail.man comment banner at the top reveals, 
the roff source is meant to be preprocessed through tbl, i. e.

nroff -man -t fetchmail.man

And grog will also figure that out:

$ grog fetchmail.man 
groff -t -man fetchmail.man

Not a bug, closing report as invalid.
